A Brief website on MINECRAFT

Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang studios in 2011. It allows players to explore a blocky, procedurally-generated 3D world, mine resources, craft items, and build structures. Due to it's open-ended nature it has various modes available to keep players engaged including but not limited to:

Survival mode
Players must gather resources, manage hunger, and fend off hostile mobs to survive. and there are many playstyles such as:
  • Singplayer - building up your own resources and base
  • Multiplayer - playing with friends (known as an SMP)
  • Hardcore mode (permanent death)
Creative mode
Players have unlimited resources and can fly, allowing them to focus on building and creating without any restrictions.
Adventure mode
Players can play custom maps and adventures created by other players with specific rules and objectives.
Modpacks
Players can install community-made modifications (mods) to add additional content, features and gameplay mechanics to the game. There are also many types of modpacks to cater for all communities
  • Tech mods - adding machines, power systems and automation
  • Magic mods - introducing spells, rituals and mystical creatures
  • Exploration mods - expanding the world with new biomes, dimensions and structures
Servers
Players can join multiplayer servers that host various game modes and mini-games, such as:
  • Skyblock - starting on a small floating island with limited resources
  • Factions - forming groups and competing for territory and resources
  • Minigames - participating in various mini-games like parkour, spleef (breaking blocks under an oponent to make them fall), and PvP battles
